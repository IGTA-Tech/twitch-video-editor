Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to process a video?

A 4-hour stream typically takes 5-15 minutes to process depending on your mode choice. FFmpeg-only is fastest (5-10 min), Hybrid takes 10-20 min, and Whisper AI takes 20-30 min.

Do I need any editing skills?

Absolutely not! Just drag your video in, pick a preset (or adjust settings if you want), and hit process. It's that simple.

What file formats are supported?

MP4, MKV, AVI, MOV, FLV, WMV, WebM, M4V, and more. Basically any video format your streaming software exports.

Is there a file size limit?

Nope! Process 14+ hour marathon streams with no issues. Cloud tools typically cap at 2-4 hours.

Does it work on Mac and Windows?

Yes! StreamCut Pro works on Windows, Mac, and Linux. We provide installers for all platforms.

Is my content uploaded to the cloud?

Never. Everything processes locally on your computer. Your content never leaves your machine. 100% private.

What's the refund policy?

30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. If it doesn't work for you, we'll refund you.
